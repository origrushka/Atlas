Arsenal Football Club

About Arsenal

Arsenal is a professional football club based in Islington, London. It is one of the most successful clubs in English football history.

An unordered list of Arsenal's biggest rivals:

  • Tottenham
  • Chelsea
  • Manchester United
  • Liverpool

An ordered list of the top of the current Premier League Table:

  • Tottenham
  • Arsenal
  • Manchester City
  • Liverpool

Martin Odegaard - Arsenal's Captain

Martin Odegaard, the talented playmaker and leader, currently serves as Arsenal's captain. His creativity and versatility make him a key player for the club. Odegaard's vision on the field and leadership skills have earned him the prestigious role of captain at Arsenal.
